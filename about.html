About Fatma:

After graduating from SQU, I spend a lot of time at home workless tring to find a dessent job. I spend two years not knowing what I what until the day where all got clear to me.

It all started in 2014 with a box of cake mix and some whipped cream when I tried to make chocolate cupcakes for my family. It wasn’t long until I discovered this was my passion and what I was meant to do. Since then, I’ve used youtube videos to learn everything I know today.


About FMA Cakery:

In 2016, I started a home business using my mother`s home cooking business. In the beginning, I started selling different flavors of cupcakes using instagram platform. Today, I make cakes and cupcakes for various occasions including:

  • Anniversaries
  • Baby Showers
  • Birthdays
  • Engagements
  • Gifts
  • Graduations
  • Special Celebration (Earth day, Eids, Mother day, National day, Teacher day and more)

I took advantage of the things I learned at the university to built my website (the one you are using now) to help me attract more customers.

Having always made cakes for my family, I thought about building on this passion to set up my own business and it wasn’t until early 2018 that dream became a reality and finally FMA Cakery was born.

I feel blessed that this business found me. When one door closes, another one opens...
